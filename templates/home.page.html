Welcome to The Productive Ramadan Android App

iOS App and website coming for Ramadan 2022 inshallah

Productive Ramadan is an app for helping us to have a productive Ramadan.
Ramadan is a time of great rewards.
The Productive Ramadan app includes a hadith a day and Quranic ayah a day page to help us learn more Islamic knowledge during the glorious month of Ramadan.
The App has a daily Ramadan task tracker so we can be accountable for the days we fast and for our Fardh, Sunnah and Tarawih prayers each day.
